The Renaissance was a period of advances in technology and science in various areas. Medicine, astronomy and geography were some of the areas which were advanced in the renaissance period. Within medicine they did experiments on the human body to extend their knowledge about it. In astronomy there were many great scientific advances, there were new theories about the solar system and there were discoveries about new stars and sunspots. The printing press was also invented in the Renaissance and it was invented by a German man named Johannes Gutenberg. The Renaissance was an era of great men such as Leonardo da Vinci, Galileo Galilei, Filippo Brunelleschi and many more.
