Marriage was starting to be an act of love but for the most part it was just a social contract, even though it was nothing romantic about the union it was still a happy grand celebration with lots of food and entertainment.<br><br>
  The women at the time got some changes in their life, not much but still some small changes. They were still supposed to cline the house, watch the children and give birth to boys but they were allowed to have their own opinions they were even expected to.<br><br>
  The food went through some development during the renaissance, not so much for the lower class but the higher class started to import new world ingredients like potatoes and chili. Another change is that they introduced forks to utensils and started to use wood, metal and later porcelain as plates instead of steal bread.
